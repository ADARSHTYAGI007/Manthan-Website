About Our Society

Founded on the principles of innovation, collaboration, and excellence, Tech Society has been at the forefront of technological advancement in our college.

Our Theory & Philosophy

Manthan: The Forte serves as the heart of student engagement and leadership on campus. Through dynamic events — including sports tournaments, cultural showcases, esports battles, and creative contests — we aim to build a community of confident, talented, and socially active students. At Manthan, we believe in empowering students to explore, express, and excel beyond academics.
